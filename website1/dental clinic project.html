    Frequently Asked Questions

    How often should I get a dental check-up?

    It is recommended to visit the dentist every 6 months for a regular dental check-up and cleaning. This helps prevent cavities, gum disease, and other oral health issues early on.

    Is teeth whitening safe and how long does it last?

    Yes, professional teeth whitening is completely safe when done under a dentist’s supervision. Results can last 6 months to 2 years, depending on your eating habits and oral hygiene.

    Is a root canal painful?

    Not at all. With modern techniques and local anesthesia, a root canal is usually painless. Most patients feel relief from pain after the first session itself.

    What is the right age to get braces?

    Braces can be done at almost any age, but the ideal age is between 10 to 14 years, when the jaw is still developing. However, adults can also get clear aligners or braces with great results.

    How long does it take to recover after a wisdom tooth removal?

    Initial recovery takes 2 to 3 days, but complete healing may take a week or more. We provide detailed post-care instructions to ensure a smooth and comfortable healing process.

    What is the best solution for missing teeth?

    Dental implants are the most natural and long-lasting solution for missing teeth. They restore both function and appearance without affecting neighboring teeth.
